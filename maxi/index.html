What is Purrfect Match?

Our job here on Purrfect Match is to find you a pet that perfectly matches your character. At first you will take a test which will make is easier for us, to find you the perfect little friend. Your test will be analyzed from our specialist, which then will choose the perfect pet for you based on your results. You will then (in the following weeks) get diffrent pictures and descriptions from diffrent animals. After you decided what animal it should be, you can come and pick it up in the Purrfect Match center that is closest to you.
